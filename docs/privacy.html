Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Overview

"Gym Rest Stopwatch" is a simple stopwatch app designed for gym rest periods. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data.

"Gym Rest Stopwatch" operates entirely on your device. The app does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Connect to the internet
  • Share any data with third parties

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
