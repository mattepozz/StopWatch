"One-Tap Stopwatch" is a simple stopwatch designed for timing rest periods between sets at the gym.

How to Use

Tap the play button to start the timer. Tap stop to reset to zero. Use the restart button to quickly reset and start again without stopping.

When running, the timer appears on your Lock Screen so you can check your rest time without unlocking your phone.
